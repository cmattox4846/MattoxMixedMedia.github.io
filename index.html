Get To Know Me

Mattox Mixed Media is a solution that I have been developing for the last 8 years. I started my career doing computer programming in the early 2000's. Since then I have had many jobs and experiances in an out of the IT world. When I joined the US Air Force I began to develop my skills in IT, as well as my skills as a photographer. As time went on I started to realize that if I wanted to build an all around solution I would need to combine all of the thing s I have learned from my private and professional life and MattoxMixedMedia was born. Since its inception I have continued to bring new services and skills to the table. I now am a certifed fullstack developer, photographer, drone pilot and all around IT enthusiest.
Everyday I am looking to continue my learning and broaden my skills in order to stay up to date on the new technolgies. I have a passion for IT and I think that if you can find that thing in life that gives you joy then you should jump on the train and ride until the tracks end.
